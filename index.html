Seven tiers of Aura

A joke metric, taken completely seriously. Where you land depends entirely on how you carry yourself — not what you say about yourself.

🔴
Negative Aura
(−)

Toxicity, drama, broken trust, and embarrassing moments. Easy to gain, hard to undo.

⚪
Neutral Aura
(0)

Nothing impressive, nothing embarrassing. The default state of most actions.

🟢
Positive Aura
(+)

Respectful, helpful, mature. The baseline worth aiming for, every day.

🔵
Elite Aura
(++)

Consistency over time. The kind of behavior people start to expect from you.

🟣
Legendary Aura
(+++)

Leadership, wisdom, and moments people bring up months later.

🟡
Infinite Aura
(∞)

A meme rank. Reserved for iconic, unrepeatable plays.

⚫
Negative Infinite Aura
(−∞)

The floor. Used jokingly — and sometimes not so jokingly — for the most foolish, indefensible moves. See Rule 69.

💡
Aura is a vibe, not a stat. No app tracks it, no leaderboard is official — it's a shared language a friend group uses to call out good and bad behavior without making it a whole thing.
